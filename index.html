|2010 winner|

Kings Speech
synopsis

Colin Firth plays the future King George VI who, to cope with a stammer, sees Lionel Logue, an Australian speech and language therapist played by Geoffrey Rush. The men become friends as they work together, and after his brother abdicates the throne, the new king relies on Logue to help him make his first wartime radio broadcast upon Britain's declaration of war on Germany in 1939.

leading actors

Colin Firth, Geoffrey Rush, Helena Bonham Carter, Guy Pearce

|2018 winner|
Green Book
synopsis

Set in 1962, the film is inspired by the true story of a tour of the Deep South by African American classical and jazz pianist Don Shirley and Italian American bouncer Frank "Tony Lip" Vallelonga who served as Shirley's driver and bodyguard. The film was based on interviews with his father and Shirley, as well as letters his father wrote to his mother.

leading actors

Viggo Mortensen, Mahershala Ali, Linda Cardellini
